Our Featured Projects

Makerspace Belgaum is a community-driven creative learning centre. Makerspace enables immersive and experiential learning through a 'learn by doing' approach. In the makerspace visitors are invited to explore a curiosity-driven exhibits, investigate a range of phenomena with artists, scientists, engineers, educators, and others by participating in a collaborative activity. Makerspace Belgaum also makes available all the tools for kids to facilitate learning by exploring and experimenting. Join us and be a part of a vibrant community where 'learning by doing' isn't just a method; it's our mantra!!!
